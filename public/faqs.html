Prospective students must provide a birth certificate, two passport-sized photos, and past academic reports. We also conduct a friendly interview or entry assessment to ensure your child is placed in the most supportive learning environment for their needs.

For your convenience, we accept payments via **M-Pesa Paybill (247247)**, Direct Bank Deposits to our Equity Bank account, and Banker's Cheques. Please visit our Fees Page for specific account details.

We strictly follow the **Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC)** as mandated by the Ministry of Education. Our approach emphasizes practical skills, values, and core competencies from Early Years through to Junior Secondary School (JSS).

We believe in personalized attention. Our classes maintain an average ratio of **1:20**, ensuring that every child receives the individual support and mentorship they need to excel academically and socially.

Holistic growth is key. We offer a wide range of activities including **Soccer, Swimming, Chess, Music (Piano & Guitar), Drama, Debate, and Art**. These sessions take place during designated afternoon hours.

Yes! We provide a nutritious mid-morning snack and a well-balanced hot lunch daily. Our menu is designed to be child-friendly yet healthy, and we cater to specific dietary requirements upon request.

Yes. We offer reliable and safe school transport across Embakasi and its environs. Our buses are well-maintained and always accompanied by a professional conductor to ensure the safety of your child during the commute.

Our gates open at **7:00 AM**. Lessons begin promptly at **8:00 AM**. Playgroup and Pre-school finish at **12:30 PM**, while Primary and Junior Secondary classes conclude at **3:30 PM**.
