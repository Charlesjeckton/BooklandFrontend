Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We’re here to help.

Prospective students must provide a birth certificate, two passport-sized photos, past academic reports (if applicable), and complete an application form. An interview or assessment may be required.

Yes. We offer safe and reliable school transport services within designated routes in Embakasi and surrounding areas. Transport fees vary based on location.

Bookland follows the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) approved by the Kenyan Ministry of Education, from Playgroup to Junior Secondary School.

Fees can be paid via bank deposit, MPesa Paybill, or directly at the school office. Check our Fees Page for payment instructions and details.

We offer a range of activities including music, drama, soccer, athletics, chess, art, and debate club to support holistic development.

Our school day begins at 7:30 AM and concludes at 3:30 PM for primary and junior secondary students. For our early childhood learners (Playgroup & PP1), the day ends at 12:30 PM, though after-school care options are available upon request.

Yes. We provide a balanced, nutritious hot lunch and a mid-morning snack. Our menu is carefully planned to ensure students receive the energy and nutrients they need for a full day of learning. We also accommodate specific dietary requirements or allergies—please notify the school nurse during admission.

We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention for every learner. Our average teacher-to-student ratio is 1:20, allowing our educators to identify and nurture each child's unique strengths and learning style.

To make life easier for parents, the school provides core textbooks on a loan basis. A detailed list of personal stationery and exercise books required for each grade is provided upon enrollment or at the end of each academic year for the following term.

Yes, all students are required to wear the official Bookland Schools uniform. This promotes a sense of equality, discipline, and school pride. Uniforms can be purchased directly from our official school outfitter located on campus.
