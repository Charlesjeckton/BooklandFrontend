Our Story

Educating Minds, Inspiring Hearts

Bookland Schools began with a simple yet powerful vision to create a nurturing space where young minds are empowered to dream, learn, and lead. From play group to junior secondary, we've grown into a vibrant learning community that values curiosity, character, and academic excellence.

2014

"Bookland Schools opened its doors with a small, dedicated team and a big vision: to provide a holistic education that treats every child as an individual."

2016

Following the success of our early years program, we expanded into the Primary school levels and opened our modern library.

2018

Bookland became a recognized name in regional co-curricular activities including music, drama, and sports.

2024

Full implementation of JSS framework with state-of-the-art science and computer laboratories.
