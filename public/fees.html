Important Note on Fees

Tuition Fees

Optional Services

A one-time, non-refundable fee is applicable for all new admissions to secure a slot.

Siblings Discount

We offer a 5% discount for the 2nd child and 10% for the 3rd and subsequent siblings enrolled concurrently.
